Science Department

HOD: Mr. T. Kwiripi

BSc Physics & Computer Science(GZU)

Mr. Kwiripi, the Head Of the Science Department attended his primary school at, Tensen Primary School. He then went to secondary and Parimuka 2 Secondary School and the Chaplin High School. After high school, he went to GZU, where he attained his BSc in Physics and Computer Science. He was a Physics teacher at Mkoba 3 High School (2015). He then joined Fletcher High School in 2016 as a Physics and Computer Science teacher. He became the HOD in 2018
